European Cricket Network

The European Cricket League, Malmö

The European Cricket League is the Champions League of European cricket. The domestic league champion clubs from the International Cricket Council (ICC) member federations of Sweden, Denmark, France, Germany, Italy, Netherlands, Romania, Russia and Spain compete to be crowned the "Kings of Cricket" in the ECL as Europe's premier club competition. In the short term, the ECL is focussed on shining as the highlight of the European cricketing calendar. In the long term, the team's target is for cricket to become Europe's number one summer bat and ball team sport. The ECL strives to ignite club and grassroots cricket around Europe, motivate current cricketers in Europe to play today, and inspire parents in Europe to introduce their children to cricket to help grow the game tomorrow. The 2019 edition of the ECL (ECL19) was viewed on live TV & streamed around the world in over 120 countries in more than 140 million households by over 40 broadcasters.

Malmöhus Cricket Club is one of the best clubs in Sweden. They have been given the hosting rights for organizing ECS Dream X1 2020 tournament from 3rd Aug - 7th Aug, 2020 in Malmö. Malmöhus has always been a great supporter of quality cricket with equal opportunities to all. We encourage all teams in Southern Sweden to show their interest in one of the best tournaments of all time. Our team of organizers, marketing, operations, software development and cricketing professionals boast over two decades of expertise.

Malmöhus Cricket Club

Malmöhus cricket Club was founded in 1993 by Samath Ohlén, Eric Folker och Ramesh Paul. The idea was that everybody who was interested to play the game of cricket was welcome to join, young and old, boys and girls. Integration between different cultures and countries is a cornerstone of our club. The club currently has members from 12 different nationalities, something that we are proud of. We welcome everyone who is interested in being a part of our club. We currently offer the following activities;

Coaching for beginners, boys and girls
Cooperation with DR cricket center in Malmö, coaching and indoor facilities ( nets, Bowling machines) all year round.
Elite team that plays around 30 matches per season ( T- 20 and 50 over format )
A second team that is mixed by youngsters and more experienced players, plays around 20 matches a season ( T-20 and 30 overs format )
Old boys cricket for those who are above 40 years old, approx 10 games per season
First ladies cricket team in the Skåne region
Ladies training happens on every Wednesday 18.30 to 20:00
Kids team ( all ages) that is growing in number
Outdoor practice Tuesdays and Thursdays from 17:30 – 20:00 at Limhamnsfältet (For senior teams during the season)
Indoor practice at DR cricket centre from October to April (for senior teams during the winter)
See other pages on the website for details of ladies and junior cricket times
Different social events all year round

Ladies Cricket


Last July Malmöhus ladies was formed. Its been an eventful first year and we have grown and have now got 18 members from 8 different countries. Back in February we took a team up to Stockholm to play in the SM indoor championships, 5 teams had entered and we came out on top. Very proud to say we are the SM indoor champions for 2020. A couple of weeks ago we hosted our first tournament, where Stockholm and Gothenburg came down and played. This was a great weekend. We are a really friendly bunch of girls/women so why don't you come down and join us. We train once a week on Wednesdays 18.30 to 20 at Limhamnsfältet in the summer and rest of the time indoors at DR Cricket Centre. Please follow us on Instagram malmohusladiescricket.
